Everything you need to know before working with us

We accept all major video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, MXF, R3D, BRAW, and ProRes. We can also work with footage from any camera system including Sony, Canon, ARRI, RED, and DJI drones. Simply upload your files via our secure transfer portal and we'll handle the rest.
